Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Shop tab and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore your premium subscription and any previously purchased items.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your app store:

  • iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Unmatched
  • Android: Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Unmatched

I lost my progress. Can you help?

If you were signed in with Google or Apple, your progress is saved to the cloud. Simply sign in again on your new device. If you were playing as a guest, progress is stored locally and cannot be transferred.

How do I delete my account?

Send us an email at partha31415@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

The app is crashing or not loading.

Try these steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the app store
  • Restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, email us with your device model and OS version
